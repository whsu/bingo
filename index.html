Click Start to begin. Each turn has 30 seconds.

At each turn of the game, two numbers are generated on the top left. Mark off up to three numbers by clicking them on the bingo card:

  • a multiple of the first number,
  • a multiple of the second number, and
  • a multiple of the sum of the two numbers.

Correct numbers are marked in green.

Incorrect numbers are marked in red and will be unavailable for one turn.
